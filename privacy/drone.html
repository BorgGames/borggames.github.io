Borg Drone Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our service. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What information do we collect?

We collect your email address when you sign up for our service. We use your email address to verify your identity, communicate with you, and provide you with access to our service. We also collect your IP address when you use our service. We use your IP address to prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access to our service. We also collect hardware configuration of your device. We use this information to match players to your device. We do not collect any other personal information from you, such as your name or location.

How do we use your information?

We use your information only for the purposes stated above. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties, except as required by law or to protect our rights and property.

How do we protect your information?

We store your information on secure servers that are protected by encryption and firewalls. We also use industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your information.

How can you access or delete your information?

You can access or delete your information at any time by logging into your account and going to the settings page. You can also contact us at support@borg.games if you have any questions or concerns about your privacy.

By using our service, you agree to this privacy policy and consent to our collection and use of your information as described above. We may update this policy from time to time and will notify you of any changes by email or through our service. Your continued use of our service after any changes means that you accept the updated policy.
